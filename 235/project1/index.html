About me

My name is Robert Reddick and my hometown is Brockport, New York. I'm a second year undergraduate student at RIT majoring in Game Design and Development. I also plan on taking on a minor in Computing Security. Ever since I was very young I have always loved computer science and have aspired to make my ideas and others ideas come to life. Throughout the years I have gained quite a bit of experience and am comfortable with using Java, Javascript, C#, Python, HTML, and CSS. I am also familiar with using Visual Studio, Monogame, Maya, Unity, and Photoshop CC.
