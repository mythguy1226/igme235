About me

Inverse Dungeon

Description: A 2D platformer game where you take a traditional dungeon game and you flip it upside down. You the player are the weak enemy trying to keep the hero from surviving the end of the dungeon. You are too weak to defeat him alone so you must retreat further into the dungeon to gather more enemies to aid you in defeating the hero. If the hero makes it to the end of the dungeon or defeats you then you lose, but if you manage to defeat the hero you win!

Marvel Character Search

Description: A wep app used for displaying characters from the Marvel Universe. This was made using Javascript and the Marvel API. It can be used to display a description of the character, as well as comics and events that character was in. You can also do more general searches by searching by what the character name starts with.
