Radioactive Run

I. High Concept

Simple Sidescroller similar to that of Geometry Dash, except in this you are also trying to escape a radiation leak while you try to reach the end of the level.

II. Genre

Gameplay Genre: Side Scroller/Platformer
IGN Genre: Action

III. Platform

Will be friendly with most screen-sizes but will mostly be available across different desktops.

IV. Story

It began when a group of scientists were doing work on a Nuclear Reactor, while they were doing this, one of the scientists tried to kill the rest of his fellow scientists by leaking out the radiation. You are one of the surviving scientists trying to escape the plant and get to safety. As you (the player) run away from the radioactive wave you face many obstacles such as debris and other scientists who conspired with the culprit to destroy you.

V. Esthetics

The overall look of this game will use simple 2D Pixel Graphics that will be similar to an arcade style game.
The sounds in this game will be simple, most likely some 8-bit and techno, and possibly some ambient sounds to help set the mood even further.
Types of sounds will include, sounds made by the player, some background sounds, and perhaps some UI sounds.

VI. Gameplay

Mechanics:
- Avoiding Obstacles
- Breaking through obstacles
- Picking up Powerups

Controls:
- Movement: WASD
- Shoot Gun: Space Bar
- Interacting with UI: Mouse
- Pause Screen: Q

Teaching the Game:
The player will be introduced with an instructions screen when they click on the start button which will force them to basically read it or acknowledge it before playing the game.

Learning the Game:
As the player goes throughout the game they will learn that they need to stay very attentive as they progress through the levels because there will be a variety of different obstacles that will require them to avoid or break through so they dont get caught by the radiation.
